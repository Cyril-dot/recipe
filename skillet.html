Skillet Pepper and Garlic Pork Chops

If you like garlic and love black pepper you'll love these skillet pork chops. Simple, yet, very tasty!

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Additional Time: 30 mins
  • Total Time: 55 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yields: 4 pork chops

Ingredients

  • 4 (1 1/2 inches thick) boneless center-cut pork chops
  • 4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons coarsely ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Combine pork chops, Worcestershire sauce, 3 tablespoons olive oil, garlic, and black pepper in a large, resealable plastic bag. Marinate chops in the mixture for about 30 minutes.
  2. Heat remaining oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Remove pork from the marinade and place into the skillet to brown on all sides, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat and let simmer, basting with marinade during cooking, until no longer pink in the centers, 15 to 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
